About Me

I am currently working as Project Scientist at the Department of Aerospace Engineering, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, with over a decade of work experience. My leadership activities include coordinating projects, mentoring junior researchers, and ensuring high standards of research output. Looking ahead, I aim to expand my research capabilities, and foster an innovative, team-oriented environment to drive significant breakthroughs in Modern engineering.

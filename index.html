About Me

I am a Project Scientist-III with nearly a decade of experience in advanced materials research. I specialize in various research areas that include Design and development of next-generation multi-functional composite materials, thermo-mechanical performance evaluation of materials and structures, Nano-material synthesis and characterization, Electro-magnetic property characterization and analysis, Additive manufacturing. I am involved in co-ordinating multiple high-impact research projects, managing work packages, deliverables and contributing to the development of innovative manufacturing technologies. My leadership activities include coordinating tasks, mentoring junior researchers, and ensuring high standards of research output. Looking ahead, I aim to, expand my research capabilities, and foster an innovative, team-oriented environment to drive significant breakthroughs in materials science and engineering.

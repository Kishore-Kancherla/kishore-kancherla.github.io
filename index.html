About Me

I am a passionate researcher and project scientist with extensive experience in advanced composite materials, aerospace engineering, and sustainable technologies. My work bridges cutting-edge research and industrial applications, addressing challenges in advanced manufacturing processes, Material performance analysis, thermo-mechanical fatigue, and material recyclability. With over nine years of experience at premier institutions like IISc, Bangalore, I have successfully coordinated and contributed to multiple funded projects by global organizations such as Boeing, Shell, SERB, ISRO, ADA, and DRDO. My expertise spans technical problem-solving, project co-ordination, grant writing and stakeholder engagement, which have led to significant advancements in materials research, development and performance analysis. Key accomplishments include the development of next generation multi-scale and multi-functional composites, scalable radar-absorbing structures, pioneering sustainable composites using bio-degradable polymers, and receiving the Best Paper Award at SAE Aerocon 2024. My international presentations, including ASME AMRGT-2019 in Berlin, Germany, reflect my commitment to advancing knowledge in the field. I am driven by a vision of creating sustainable and innovative solutions for aerospace and industrial challenges, leveraging my skills in project coordination, advanced manufacturing, and process optimization. Let’s connect to explore synergies in research, innovation, and sustainability.
